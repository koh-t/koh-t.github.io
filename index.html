E-mail: x@i.kyoto-u.ac.jp where x is takeuchi Biography I received my B.S and M.C. in 2009 and 2011 from Waseda University, Prof. Noboru Murata in Engineering. My M.C work focused on sparse coding techniques and time series analysis for Brain Computer Interface. After finishing my M.C, I started working at Learning and Intelligent Systems Research Group of NTT CS Labs. I joined Ueda Research Laboratory of NTT CS Labs on April 2016. My current research interests are matrix and tensor factorization techniques and spatio-temporal data analysis.
